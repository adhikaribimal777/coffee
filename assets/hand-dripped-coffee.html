Hand-Dripped Coffee

Hand-dripped coffee, also known as pour-over coffee, is a brewing method that involves slowly pouring hot water over ground coffee in a filter. This meticulous process allows for precise control over extraction, resulting in a clean, nuanced, and flavorful cup.

At Komorebi Cafe, we carefully select premium single-origin beans and employ expert baristas who master the pour-over technique. The water temperature, pour rate, and grind size are all meticulously calibrated to bring out the unique characteristics of each bean, offering you a truly exceptional coffee experience.
